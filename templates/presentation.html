About

Training Process

Including the GoogLeNet model alongside the Custom CNN and Dense Neural Network (DNN), the training process involves diverse architectures. The labeled chest X-ray dataset for 'COVID', 'NORMAL' and 'PNEUMONIA' is input into each model. With specialized convolutional or dense layers, the models discern intricate patterns. The training optimizes parameters using categorical crossentropy loss and the Adam optimizer. Gradually, the models fine-tune their weights, improving their accuracy in classifying respiratory conditions in unseen X-ray images.

Enables early identification of respiratory conditions, improving intervention timing and enhancing overall patient outcomes.

Rapid and accurate classifications support quick healthcare assessments, aiding timely treatment and resource allocation.

Automated image analysis streamlines diagnostics, optimizing healthcare resources and allowing focus on critical tasks for practitioners.
